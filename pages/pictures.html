A few pictures I took

The famous Torri gate on Miyajima (or Itsukushima as it's more formally known as) just off the coast of Hiroshima on Honshu (did you know that Japan consists of four "main" islands?).

A wild blackbear I encountered when I hiked the Sahale Arm in the Northern Cascades. He (or she) was foraging for berries and other goodies around the trail. The bear also went for a dip in a nearby pond. It was a really cool thing to witness in person!
